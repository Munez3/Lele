About Lele

LeLe Crossmedia Productions is one of the first and youngest partnerships in Poland, incorporating international points of view, endeavors, passions, and love for the art of cinema, photography and animation; for the opportunities and possibilities of cross media projects. We pride in our unity and team work in accomplishing our mission.

While, our main aim is to promote cross media projects we also undertake the production and co-production of the desired film and animation attributes. LeLe Productions helps to explore the new cross media areas and encourages implementing the group work by its colorful and innovative disposition.
